Intermediate

The Intermediate Courses are taught at the Government or Private or Cooperative Junior Colleges for two years. These Courses are followed by prestigious State level Entrance Tests for admission to the various Degree Colleges offering Professional or general education.

Degree

Degree Course means a course of study at a recognised higher education institution of at least three years full-time duration, or its part-time equivalent. Based on 6 documents. 6. Degree Course means a university or college of advanced education degree course approved by the Director.
